A little about me

A little about me...I’m married to my best friend and have two munchkins who fill our home with laughter and mischief. We also have a co-dependent Pomeranian who lies about going potty and loves to chase the vacuum. Throw in a couple of fish, turtle and a hamster and that’s our family. I am a part-time teacher by day and an artist by night. I guess you could say my life is filled with magic every minute of the day.

I have a strong passion for art. All forms. Growing up I was given free rein to work with many mediums of art and was encouraged in any pursuit I chose. I started with drawing and painting, enjoyed working as a scrapbook designer and teacher and finally found my love....photography and digital art. I can’t wait for the next adventure.

If you're interested in a custom-designed print for an event send me a convo, I’ll be happy to design it for you.
